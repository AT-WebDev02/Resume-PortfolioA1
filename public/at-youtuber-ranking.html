Some of my favourite YouTubers

1. LazerPig

LazerPig is an alcoholic British (specifically Scottish) YouTuber, whose videos are debunking histoical military myths (especially myths that's accepted as current historical fact by the general public, example the T-34 is not as good as people suggests), with the dry self-deprication humour that British people are well-known for.

2. martincitopants

Martincitopants is an Australian gaming YouTuber gaming channel, whose videos being well-edited, well-humoured (according to me, myself and I), sometimes informative, and potentially, Martin suffering (in video games).

3. mikeburnfire

Mikeburnfire are American let's play YouTubers, their videos being Mike and Zach talking about their life experiences whilst playing video games, with Zach usually talking about his time and experiences enlisted in the US Army as a smallarms repairman.
